How to Use


GuardNAngel seeks to decrease the time between overdose and medical services by monitoring your heart rate and automatically sending Emergency Medical Services to your location if it ever drops to a critical level. By calling EMS as soon as a critical heart rate is detected, the greater the chance of survival and less damage sustained from the overdose.

First connect your monitor to the app through Bluetooth. Once you are connected press the start button to run the app.

If your heart ever falls below a critical level, a warning will flash on screen alerting you to notify the app whether you are okay. Just press the button on screen to dismiss the alarm. If you do not press the button within a certain time limit, EMS will be sent directly to your location.

Add an emergency contact so that they will be notified if you ever fail to dismiss the emergency alert.
